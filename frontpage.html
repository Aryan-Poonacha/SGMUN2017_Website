Welcome to SGMUN!

Welcome to the fifth edition of Sishu Griha Model United Nations! After a one-year hiatus, the only conference in Bangalore that caters almost exclusively to the first-time delegates is back for another session. Our main purpose is to identify budding talent and provide them with a platform to hone their skills. We hope to inculcate in the participants debating skills, diplomacy, analytical thinking and most importantly an interest in international affairs at an early stage. With one of the best Executive Boards on the circuit, Sishu Griha MUN 2017 will be an experience you do not want to miss.
We look forward to your presence at SGMUN 2017!
